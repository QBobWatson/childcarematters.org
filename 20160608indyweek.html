Indyweek Best of the Triangle Finalist

Childcare Matters was honored to be a finalist for Best Early Childhood Learning Facility in Orange/Chatham County in IndyWeek’s Best of the Triangle edition.

The annual contest sponsored by the popular weekly publication racked up more than 600,000 votes from more than 20,000 voters in over 300 categories. Finalists were chosen after a month-long public nomination process with the top four nominees moving into a final voting round.

The recognition wasn’t the first for Childcare Matters. It has been a finalist in the IndyWeek awards in previous years and was named Best Daycare in Chapel Hill for 2012 and 2013 by the Chapel Hill News Rose Awards.

Thanks to the more than 30 years of families, alumni, and friends who voted for us!
